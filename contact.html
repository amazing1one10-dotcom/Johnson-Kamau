Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about working with our design team.

What's your typical design process?

Our process begins with a discovery call to understand your vision, followed by research, concept development, design execution, and iterative refinements. We maintain transparent communication throughout to ensure the final product exceeds expectations.

How long does a typical project take?

Timeline varies based on project scope. A brand identity might take 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive website design could take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and work diligently to meet agreed-upon deadlines.

What are your pricing structures?

We offer both fixed-price packages for common projects and custom quotes for unique needs. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. We'll provide a detailed proposal after understanding your project requirements and budget.

Do you offer ongoing design support?

Yes! We offer retainer packages for clients who need continuous design support. These can include a set number of hours per month, priority access to our team, and discounted rates for ongoing work.
